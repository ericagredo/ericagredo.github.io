About me

Get to know who I am!

Hey, I’m Eric!

    I am a Software Engineer graduated from the University of Florida. My main interests are in iOS development where my passion is to create a wonderful user experience through my code. My interests for iOS development began when I worked on the World’s First Brain Drone Race. Furthermore, I worked at MIT Media Lab for two years where I worked on a new data security protocol called Open Algorithm Project (OPAL). I also developed an Infomediary iOS application where personal datastores were created and easily accessible.

    I love to apply an interdisciplinary attitude to every project I create because of the variety of interests I have. Finally, I am extremely passionate about nature and star gazing. I love to find inspiration, peace, and humility by the mere observation of our world and universe. This has led me to create a nonprofit called Project Nature, Inc. where our mission is to expose underprivileged youth in urban areas to the benefits of nature. We combine the basics of programming through Scratch and nature to create awesome stories.
